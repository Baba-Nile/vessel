Admissions Information

Everything you need to know to start your application and join our community of skilled professionals.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of National ID or Birth Certificate
  • Academic transcripts or certificates
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Payment of application fee

Application Process

  1. Choose your desired course.
  2. Complete the online form on the Apply page.
  3. Attach required documents.
  4. Pay the application fee.
  5. Receive confirmation via call or email.
